ABOUT US

CITY TALK CLUB is a non-profit higher ed community in pursuit of building a sophisticated network of undergrads, graduates and post graduates to facilitate them with expertise and guidance from top leaders of their respective industries. CITY TALK CLUB provides a one-stop platform for students to connect with peers, leverage alumni networks, learn correct strategies to pursue career, remain updated by myth busters, apply for internships and grow as an individual.

MENTORSHIP

Get insightful knowledge on various topics ranging from fully funded international scholarships, research internships to building startup, exploring hybrid careers and much more.

RESOURCES

Get a chance to earn the resources presented in the session directly in your inbox, including ppt, links, and also recorded sessions.

NETWORKING

Get resourceful connections to build a strong network and also get an opportunity to leverage your network to build a strong community of like minded individuals.
